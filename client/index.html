Split Restaurant Bills Instantly

EasySplit makes it easy to divide restaurant bills with friends. No signup required - just create a split, add your items, and share a link. Everyone can see who owes what in real-time.

How It Works

  1. Start a new bill split - no account needed
  2. Add everyone who's splitting the bill
  3. Add menu items and assign them to people
  4. Share the link so others can join live
  5. See the final breakdown of who owes what

Features

  • Real-time collaboration - everyone sees updates instantly
  • Split items between multiple people
  • Add service charge and tip percentages
  • Save and reuse restaurant menus
  • Works on any device - phone, tablet, or desktop
  • Completely free, no login required
